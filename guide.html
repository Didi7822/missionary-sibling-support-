Ways You Can Help

Every act of generosity — big or small — makes Almu and Didi's missionary year possible. Here are the most meaningful ways you can partner with them.

Give a One-Time Gift

The most direct way to help. Every dollar goes straight to funding Almu and Didi's year of apostolic service with Regnum Christi.

Connect Them to Your Parish

A personal testimony at Mass is one of the most powerful fundraising tools Almu and Didi have. If you have a connection to a pastor or parish coordinator, an introduction from you could open the door for a talk that raises thousands.

What to say: "I know two young missionaries raising money for a Regnum Christi missionary year — could they speak at Mass one weekend?"
